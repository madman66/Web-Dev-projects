Meet Our Star Players

Virat Kohli

Role: Top-order Batsman (Right-hand bat)

Born: November 5, 1988, Delhi, India

Career Highlights:

  • Over 70 international centuries, including 27 in Tests and 43 in ODIs.
  • Played a pivotal role in India's victories in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Rohit Sharma

Role: Opening Batsman (Right-hand bat)

Born: April 30, 1987, Bansod, Maharashtra, India

Career Highlights:

  • Record-holder for the highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs).
  • Led India to victories in multiple ICC tournaments, including the ICC Champions Trophy 2013.

MS Dhoni

Role: Wicketkeeper-Batsman (Right-hand bat)

Born: July 7, 1981, Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), India

Career Highlights:

  • Led India to victory in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
  • Retired from international cricket in 2019 but continues to play in the Indian Premier League.

Hardik Pandya

Role: All-rounder (Right-hand bat, Right-arm fast-medium)

Born: October 11, 1993, Choryasi, Gujarat, India

Career Highlights:

  • Known for his explosive batting and useful bowling in limited-overs formats.
  • Plays for Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League and represents India in all formats.

KL Rahul

Role: Wicketkeeper-Batsman (Right-hand bat)

Born: April 18, 1992, Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Career Highlights:

  • A consistent performer for India across all formats.
  • Plays for the Punjab Kings in the Indian Premier League and Karnataka in domestic cricket.

Jasprit Bumrah

Role: Fast Bowler (Right-arm fast)

Born: December 6, 1993, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India

Career Highlights:

  • Known for his deadly yorkers and ability to bowl in the death overs.
  • A key player in India’s bowling attack, particularly in limited-overs formats.

Shikhar Dhawan

Role: Opening Batsman (Left-hand bat)

Born: December 5, 1985, Delhi, India

Career Highlights:

  • Known for his solid opening partnerships and aggressive batting style.
  • A consistent performer for India in ODI and T20 cricket.
